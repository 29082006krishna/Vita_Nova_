Analytix is an AI-powered retail intelligence platform that helps businesses forecast product demand accurately and make smarter decisions. Using Artificial Intelligence, Generative AI, and advanced forecasting models, Analytix converts complex retail data into clear and actionable insights. Retail demand changes due to customer behavior, seasonal trends, and promotions, while traditional systems often rely on static and delayed analysis. Analytix unifies retail data, delivers real-time forecasts, and uses Generative AI to explain results, support what-if scenarios, and guide inventory and planning decisions. Our goal is to reduce stock inefficiencies, lower costs, and improve customer satisfaction.
